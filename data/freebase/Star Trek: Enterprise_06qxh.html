The show is set in the nearby regions of the Milky Way galaxy around the year 2150, over a century before the original Star Trek series. It is set aboard the starship Enterprise NX-01, Earth's first warp 5 capable ship which was designed for long-range exploration of the galaxy, captained by Jonathan Archer.

The show is set in the nearby regions of the Milky Way galaxy around the year 2150, over a century before the original Star Trek series.
